Why Website Costs Vary in Cape Town

The cost of building a website depends on multiple factors, including design complexity, functionality, and the size of your business. Whether you need a simple brochure website or a fully custom solution, understanding pricing helps you plan your budget.

Lalo Web Agency provides professional, affordable web design for Cape Town businesses of all sizes, with clear pricing and no hidden fees.

Factors Affecting Website Costs

  • Design & Layout: Custom designs cost more than templates.
  • Number of Pages: More pages require additional design and content work.
  • Functionality: Features like booking systems, e-commerce, and forms increase cost.
  • SEO & Optimization: On-page SEO, speed optimization, and mobile responsiveness.
  • Maintenance & Hosting: Ongoing support, security, and hosting plans.
  • Content Creation: Professional copywriting, images, and branding assets.
